Inside the AI Decision Process

Our system utilizes the Random Forest Algorithm. It constructs an ensemble of 200 individual Decision Trees. Each tree analyzes your clinical data independently and casts a "vote" for the risk category. The final probability is the consensus across this entire forest.

Technical Flow:
  1. User inputs mapped to 30+ features.
  2. Missing values handled via safe defaults.
  3. Random Forest votes across 200 trees.
  4. Probability score calculated (0-100%).
  5. Final Classification based on 10% sensitivity.
